ℹ️ About Contribution Draw

Our Mission

Contribution Draw provides a secure and transparent platform for contribution groups to conduct fair monthly beneficiary draws while maintaining a permanent record of all completed selections.

Features

  • ✅ Secure Google Authentication
  • ✅ One participant can select only one month
  • ✅ Administrator Dashboard
  • ✅ Hall of Transparency
  • ✅ Notice Board
  • ✅ Progress Tracker
  • ✅ Participant Statistics
  • ✅ CSV Export
  • ✅ Firebase Authentication
  • ✅ Firebase Firestore Database

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my selected month?

No. Once confirmed, your selected month cannot be changed.


Can one participant select more than one month?

No. Every participant is limited to one month.


Who manages the draw?

Only the administrator has permission to manage or reset the draw.
